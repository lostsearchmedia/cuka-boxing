ORIGINS

Welcome to Cuka Boxing Promotions, where passion for boxing meets unparalleled expertise. Founded by Ardian Cuka, a dedicated leader in the Utah boxing community, we are committed to elevating the sport by organizing premier boxing events that captivate both fighters and fans.

ARDIAN CUKA

Founder | Owner | President of Utah Boxing

Ardian Cuka is a driving force in the world of amateur and professional boxing, known for his tireless dedication to growing the sport across Utah and beyond. As the founder of Cuka Boxing Promotions and current President of Utah Boxing, Ardian has played a pivotal role in shaping the region's boxing landscape through over two decades of leadership and innovation.

Since 2009, Ardian has served as Chief of Officials and Vice President of the Utah Local Boxing Committee (LBC), where he has overseen more than 250 local events and 60+ regional and national championships sanctioned by USA Boxing. His deep commitment to integrity, fairness, and excellence has earned him the respect of athletes, coaches, and officials nationwide.

In the past two years alone, Ardian has successfully organized over 35 live events, demonstrating a rare blend of organizational acumen and passion for the sport. Now, with his eyes set on the future, Ardian is leading an ambitious push to elevate Utah boxing to the national stage. His vision includes large-scale mega-events with capacities ranging from 12,000 to 23,000 attendees, global pay-per-view access, and ticketing structures that mirror the world's biggest fight nights.

Backed by in-depth market research and a keen understanding of demographic trends — particularly the rising enthusiasm for boxing among young adults — Ardian sees enormous potential for expansion. With Utah's population nearing 4 million and an underserved base of boxing fans, the foundation is set for explosive growth.

Whether mentoring the next generation of fighters, coordinating national championships, or planning headline events at major venues like the Maverik Center and Delta Center, Ardian remains a champion for boxing's future.

NELSON HYSA

"The Albanian Eagle"
WBO Heavyweight Champion

Nelson began his boxing career in 2000 at the first cadet championship in the city of Pogradec, where he was declared champion. Following that championship, he won many consecutive age-group championships.

In 2003, Nelson made history for Albanian boxing by winning the European medal, ranking 3rd in Europe — a success and achievement never before accomplished in Albania!

He holds the record for the most championship titles, being a 23-time absolute champion of Albania and a multiple winner of Balkan and world tournaments.

In 2022, Hysa transitioned to professional boxing, where his impressive record continues to grow. He is the only Albanian boxer to have become a European Champion and currently holds the title in professional boxing.

With a career total of 24 matches, he boasts an undefeated record of 24 wins, 0 losses, and 0 draws.

IRELAND ACOSTA

LBC Treasurer

Ireland Acosta is a USA-certified boxing coach with a passion for the sport and a mission to make a lasting impact. With years of experience in the amateur circuit, she has cornered fighters across multiple states and trained professional athletes internationally in wellness and breathwork.

As the treasurer of Utah's local boxing community, Ireland actively supports the growth in boxing wherever she goes. Specializing in yoga, breathwork, movement, and competition prep, she integrates holistic training to enhance both physical and mental performance. Her journey — marked by resilience, including overcoming a concussion during fight prep — reflects her deep commitment to boxing.

Believing boxing is a space for self-expression and empowerment, especially for women, Ireland is dedicated to helping fighters unlock their full potential. Her philosophy is simple: boxing never lies to you, as long as you never lie to it.

ERICK ACOSTA

Head Coach & LBC Secretary

With just under a decade of experience as a head coach of his boxing gym and five years as a head corner within USA boxing, Erick Acosta is a dedicated leader in the boxing community. As the current LBC Secretary, he plays a vital role in the sport's development while continuing to inspire athletes from all ages and backgrounds.

Before stepping into coaching, Erick competed for two years, acquiring six fights and gaining firsthand experience of what it means to be in the ring. His boxing journey has taken him across various states in the U.S. and Mexico, giving him a diverse perspective on the sport. Being bilingual, he connects with a wide range of athletes, ensuring that every fighter feels understood and supported.

Whether coaching amateurs or professionals, Erick's passion extends beyond boxing — he genuinely cares about every fighter's personal journey. The moment you meet him, you'll feel his unwavering support, not just as a coach, but as a friend invested in your success.

DEVON HICKS

Athletic Boxers Director of Wellness & Treatments

Devon Hicks brings over two decades of bedside nursing experience to the corner of the ring. A graduate student training to become a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner, he merges clinical insight with compassionate care for athletes fighting battles both within and outside the cage.

Beyond the clinic, Devon serves as a professional cutman and certified powerlifting and nutrition coach, helping athletes push their limits with discipline, purpose, and integrity. He believes every fighter deserves support that strengthens not only their body, but also their mind and soul — whether they're battling addiction, trauma, self-doubt, or an opponent across the canvas.

A man of deep faith, Devon is grounded by his dedicated wife and two children. In every corner of his life — the clinic, the gym, the ring, and the home — his purpose remains the same: to help people rise, rebuild, and rediscover the strength within them.
